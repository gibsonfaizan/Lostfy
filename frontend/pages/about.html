About LostFy

We're on a mission to make losing things a temporary inconvenience—not a permanent loss. Powered by AI, built with care.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of belongings go unclaimed simply because there's no reliable way to connect finders with owners. LostFy bridges that gap with artificial intelligence.

We believe that technology should serve humanity's everyday problems. A lost wallet, a misplaced phone, or a forgotten bag shouldn't have to stay lost forever.

🤖

AI-First Approach

Our platform uses CLIP for visual matching, YOLO for object detection, EasyOCR for text extraction, and FAISS for lightning-fast vector search across millions of reports.

Why Choose LostFy?

Privacy First

Faces, phone numbers, ID cards, and sensitive text are auto-blurred before any image is publicly displayed.

Location-Smart

Verified Claims

⚡

Real-Time Matches

As soon as a found item is uploaded, our FAISS engine scans the entire lost-item database in milliseconds.

🛡️

Anti-Fraud Engine

Behavioral analysis and proof verification detect and flag suspicious claims before they reach the finder.

📊

Trust Scoring
